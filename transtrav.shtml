Transportation & Travel

Fishersville, VA is located in the Shenandoah Valley of Virginia.  It is about ½ hour from Charlottesville, VA and ½ hour from Harrisonburg, VA.

Our hotel block is reserved at the Hampton Inn in Fishersville, located at 15 Four Square Lane. Check out our Accommodations page for more details!

Airports

The closest regional airport to Fishersville is Charlottesville (CHO).  The airport is about 45 minutes away from the vineyard and Staunton.

Dulles International Airport (IAD), located south of Washington, DC, is about a 2 ½ hour drive to the north, and Richmond International (RIC) is about two hours east of Fishersville.

Directions to Barren Ridge Vineyards

From I-64:
Take Exit 91 off of I-64 (Fishersville). Turn onto Tinkling Spring Road heading towards Fishersville. Turn left on Route 250 West. Just past the Food Lion turn right on Barren Ridge Road and follow for about 3 miles. (The last mile of the road is unpaved- don't worry, you haven't missed it!)  The winery is on the left.

From I-81:
Take Exit 222 off of I-81 (Fishersville). Turn onto Route 250 East, heading towards Fishersville, follow 250 for 3.5 miles. Just before the Food Lion turn left on Barren Ridge Road and follow for about 3 miles. (The last mile of the road is unpaved- don't worry, you haven't missed it!)  The winery is on the left.
